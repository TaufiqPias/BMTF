Welcome to the Bid Maker Tool - For Property Preservation and Rehab

This tool is developed to help property preservation processors to work faster and with precision. I hope it helps you all. The bid inventory will be updated continuously. If you are unable to find a bid in the inventory, please feel free to submit a Report.

BENEFITS

Faster Bid Preparing ♦ Low Chance Of Error ♦ All Bids In One Place ♦ Easy To Use

Auto hotkey reduces processing time to 36% but that requires the processors to memorize hotkeys. Bid Maker Tools reduces processing time to 78% and increases accuracy to 95%. No Memorizing Needed.

HOW TO USE

  1. Search the Categories for your services.
  2. Go to the page and fill out the form.
  3. Generate as many BID as you like.
  4. Make sure to copy all the bids (or individual bids) on a Notepad/Word.
  5. Enjoy

If you have any questions, feel free to reach out to me.
