1. Air Freshener

Purpose: Masks odors with a pleasant fragrance.
How It Works: Releases perfumes or scents to cover up bad smells (does not eliminate odors).
Best For: Quick freshness in cars, bathrooms, or homes.

2. Odor Neutralizer

Purpose: Eliminates odors by chemically binding to odor molecules.
How It Works: Uses compounds (e.g., cyclodextrins, zeolites) to trap and neutralize smells.
Best For: Persistent odors (pet smells, smoke, cooking).

3. Deodorizer

Purpose: Destroys odor-causing bacteria/molecules.
How It Works: Uses enzymes, oxidizers, or antimicrobial agents to break down odors.
Best For: Strong organic smells (trash, mildew, urine).
