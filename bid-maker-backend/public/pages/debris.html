Be very careful in taking debris measurement. Add 5-7 cyds of additional debris in case of large amount to be on the safe side. Fill out the form accordingly to generate a bid. The following prices are provided as a ranged parameter taken from RepairBase 2024.

Prices: Approximate Range

How to correctly prepare a debris bid
1. Without Dumpster: Calculate the amount of debris. Multiply the removal price according to the client type.
For Example: After calculation, total amount of debris is 35 cyds for FHA client. Price will be: $1,400.00

2. With Dumpster: Calculate the amount of debris. Multiply the moving price according to the client type. Add the dumpster price.
For Example: After calculation, total amount of debris is 35 cyds for FHA client. Debris moving price is $20/cyds and the 4 ton dumpster price is $899.99. Price will be: $1,599.99
